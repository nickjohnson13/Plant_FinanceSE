5. TheoryÂ¶

The theory for the models in this software are based directly on the work described in the reference for the NREL Cost and Scaling Model [1]. The NREL Cost and Scaling Model provides a simple cost and sizing tool to estimate wind plant cost of energy based on a small number of input parameters such as rotor diameter, hub height and rated power. The models here extract the financial calculators from the model as stand-alone modules.

Bibliography

[1]L. Fingersh, M. Hand, and A. Laxson. Wind turbine design cost and scaling model. NREL/TP-500-40566, National Renewable Energy Laboratory, Golden, CO, December 2005.
